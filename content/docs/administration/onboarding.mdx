Onboarding is the short set of fields a new member completes before they can post. Use it to gate posting on the things your community needs: an age confirmation, agreement to your terms, or a question of your own. You manage it from **Onboarding** in the admin area.

## Field types

- **Age confirmation**
- **Terms of Service acceptance**, with a link to your terms
- **Newsletter email**
- **Custom text**, a free-text answer
- **Custom select**, a choice from options you set
- **Custom checkbox**

## Per field

Each field has a **label** and an optional **description**, and you choose whether it's **required**. Terms-of-service fields also take the URL of your terms. Add fields, order them with the up and down controls, and a member completes them in that order before their first post.
