This section is for whoever runs a Barazo forum. The tool is the same no matter how the forum is hosted, so the forum-management and moderation pages here apply to everyone. The parts that differ, installing and maintaining a server versus billing and domains, live in the two hosting sub-sections at the bottom.

## Becoming an administrator

The first person to finish the setup wizard on a new forum becomes its administrator. From there you reach the admin area from your account menu once you're signed in as that account.

## What you can manage

- [Categories](/docs/administration/categories): create, nest, and order the sections your forum is organized into
- [Topics and moderation](/docs/administration/topics-and-moderation): lock, pin, and remove content, review held posts, and act on accounts
- [Rules](/docs/administration/rules): write and version your community's rules
- [Branding](/docs/administration/branding): logos, favicon, and colors
- [Content maturity](/docs/administration/content-maturity): mark and filter mature content
- [Onboarding](/docs/administration/onboarding): the fields new members complete before posting

## General settings

A few forum-wide settings live in the admin **Settings** page rather than a section of their own: the community name and description, the reaction style members see ([likes, votes, or emoji](/docs/using/features/reactions)), the community's default maturity rating, and how deeply replies can nest.

## Hosting

How you keep the forum running depends on where it lives:

- [Self-hosting](/docs/administration/self-hosted): run it on your own server with Docker
- [Managed hosting](/docs/administration/saas): billing, domains, and settings for a hosted forum
