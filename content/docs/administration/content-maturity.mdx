---
title: Content Maturity
description: Mark and filter mature content on a Barazo forum with three maturity levels.
---

Barazo has three maturity levels, and they let you keep a forum, or parts of it, behind a maturity gate. The levels are **safe**, **mature**, and **adult**.

## Where maturity is set

Maturity is set in two places:

- **The forum default**, on the admin Settings page. It's the baseline for the whole forum.
- **Per category**, in the [category](/docs/administration/categories) settings. A category can be more mature than the forum default, never less.

The **Content ratings** page in the admin area gives you a read-only overview: the three levels, your forum's current rating, and each category's rating in one place.

## How the levels interact

- A category can't be marked below the forum default. If the forum is "mature", its categories are mature or adult.
- Raising the forum-wide default is blocked while any category still sits below the new level. Move those categories up first.
- Setting a forum to mature or adult affects how it appears in the wider Barazo network's aggregated views.

## What members see

Depending on the forum's setup and their own preference, members see mature content behind a warning or filtered out. Their side of this is covered in [Staying safe](/docs/using/features/safety).
