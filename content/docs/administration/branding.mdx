---
title: Branding
description: Set your forum's logos, favicon, and colors on a Barazo forum.
---

You can make a forum look like your community from the admin **Design** page. Branding covers images and colors. The community name itself is set on the Settings page.

## Images

Upload and remove each of these, in JPEG, PNG, WebP, or GIF:

- **Header logo**, a wide mark for the top bar (up to 600 by 120 pixels)
- **Community logo**, a square mark (up to 512 by 512 pixels)
- **Favicon**, the small icon browsers show on tabs (up to 256 by 256 pixels)

A **Show community name in header** toggle controls whether your forum's name appears next to the logo.

## Colors

Set a **primary** and an **accent** color, each as a hex value (like `#24837B`). They carry through the forum's interface.
