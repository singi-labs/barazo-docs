This is the managed-hosting side of running a Barazo forum: the billing, domains, and account parts that come with letting us host it. The day-to-day management of the forum itself (categories, moderation, branding, and the rest) is the same wherever it's hosted, and lives under [Administration](/docs/administration).

## Getting started

Your Barazo forum is fully managed, so there are no servers to maintain and no Docker to configure. Sign in to your admin dashboard to manage everything.

## In this section

- **Quick start**: your first 10 minutes after creating a forum
- **Billing and subscriptions**: plans, invoices, upgrading, downgrading, VAT
- **Custom domains**: connect your own domain to your Barazo forum
- **Usage and limits**: what's included in your plan and what happens at the limits
- **Managed backups**: how backups work and how to request a restore
- **Data export**: export your data or migrate away
- **Support**: how to get help and expected response times

## Managing the forum itself

Running the community (categories, topics, moderation, rules, branding, maturity, onboarding) works the same whether you use managed hosting or self-host. See [Administration](/docs/administration).
